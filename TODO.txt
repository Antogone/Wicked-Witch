Hexes and Curses

Targeted Hexes: Allow the Witch to hex individual mobs with unique effects:

Stone Hex: Turns enemies into immobile stone statues temporarily.
Greed Hex: Makes mobs drop extra loot but attack each other in a frenzy.
Decay Curse: Reduces enemy health gradually over time.
Curse Zones: Create areas where mobs are weakened or slowed.




Nature Manipulation

Thorn Barriers: Summon a wall of thorny vines to block attacks.
Overgrowth: Cause plants to grow rapidly, entangling enemies or creating natural structures.
Fungal Spreads: Infest the area with mushrooms that emit poisonous spores.



Astral Magic

Moon Cycle Magic:
Full Moon: Spells are more powerful, and certain mobs (like wolves) become allies.
New Moon: Access shadow-based abilities, like teleportation or invisibility.
Starfall: Call down shooting stars to damage enemies or heal allies.
Time Manipulation: Temporarily speed up or slow down mobs.



2. Witch’s Lair Customization

Lair Themes

Allow players to choose themes for their lair (e.g., forested hideout, swamp coven, or fiery volcano) with unique bonuses:
Swamp Coven: Increased potion effects and nature magic.
Volcanic Lair: Stronger fire spells and resistance to explosions.
Arctic Refuge: Slows enemies entering the lair and boosts frost magic.
Resource Automation

Enchanted tools in the lair can automatically:
Harvest plants for potions.
Mine special ores for crafting.
Collect mob drops from a trap room.
